ABOUT US

Founded on February 1, 1992 by Srinivas INTERBUS (popularly known as INTERBUS NANI), INTERBUS TOURS & TRAVELS is the largest provider of Inter-City bus transportation, serving more than 75 destinations with about 425 schedules daily across Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Maharashtra and Tamilnadu. Under the able leadership of INTERBUS Srinivas who was well mentored by his grand father late SRI INTERBUS VENKAIAH, the first and foremost bus operator in Andhra Pradesh, since 1928, INTERBUS TOURS & TRAVELS became an Icon providing SAFE, ENJOYABLE and AFFORDABLE LUXURY TRAVEL to around 15 lakh people each year. INTERBUS is one of the most recognized and reputed brands in South India.

While INTERBUS is well known for its regularity and on-time performance, its group companies also provide value added services to its customers. INTERBUS CARGO CARRIERS offers value-priced overnight parcel delivery to all its destinations. INTERBUS delivers sooner than any other carrier in it’s sector.

The company’s tour service offers charter and tour packages for business, conventions, schools and other groups at affordable rates.

INTERBUS’S new generation fleet is backed up by cutting edge technology, well trained man power and infrastructure to ensure the highest standards of operating efficiency. With excellent maintenance support by INTERBUS’S own state of the art technology workshops, there will be no compromise on safety, reliability, comfort and on time travel.

Be it the management, the fleet, the ground staff or the bus crew focus on performance and customer satisfaction. Every INTERBUS employee is groomed to be smart, friendly, proficient and well-informed ensuring that their interaction will make the passengers feel welcomed and looked after. The drivers are well trained to make the journey a safe and comfortable one.

Indeed we are “Driven by Dedication“
